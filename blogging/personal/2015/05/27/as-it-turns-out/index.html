As it turns out...

May 27, 2015
1 minute read

I’ve been quiet for a long time. I’ve made some half arsed attempts previously to start writing again, but it ended up being too much of an effort, so I just let it fade into obscurity.

I’ve decided it is time for another attempt, but this time I’ll do it for me. I’ll write about anything. I’ve never actually wrote about life, the universe and all that crap.

I think I might enjoy it.

Just forgive the odd technical post that may or may not pop up, as old habits die hard.
